Cookies Policy


Last updated: May 2025


At Tech Port, your privacy and online experience matter to us. This Cookies Policy explains how we use cookies on our website, what they do, and how you can manage them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login details, and browsing activity to improve your experience.


How Tech Port Uses Cookies

Tech Port uses cookies for the following reasons:


  • Essential Cookies

    • These are necessary for the proper functioning of the Tech Port website. Without them, you might not be able to use certain features like secure login or navigation.

  • Performance & Analytics Cookies

    • These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Tech Port may use tools like Google Analytics to analyze anonymous usage data to improve website performance and user experience.

  • Third-Party Cookies

    • Tech Port may embed content from third parties (such as videos, maps, or social media), which might set their own cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies, but we only use trusted services.

Managing Cookies


When you visit Tech Port, you may be given the option to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can also control cookies at any time through your browser settings.
Please note that disabling some cookies may affect the functionality of the Tech Port website.

Changes to This Policy


Tech Port may update this Cookies Policy occasionally to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
